Ice Bear

Ice Bear is an ISTP personality type. He is practical and is drawn to understanding how things work. Freedom is important to people of this personality type and he shies away from situations where he feels tied down. As an ISTP, he is observant of his environment and notices even the smallest of changes.

Backstory:

It was shown in "Yuri and the Bear" that Ice Bear had lived in the Arctic (specifically Siberia) alone until he met a man named Yuri after bumping into one of the poles holding up his tent while evading a pack of hunting dogs.
